## Project Overview

UniBuddy is a student-focused website designed to help university and college students manage their academic life more effectively. The platform aims to support students by providing tools for study planning, collaboration, and academic success.

The UniBuddy website introduces students to available services and provides a user-friendly platform for accessing academic support and information.

---

## Organisation Overview

UniBuddy is designed to assist students in managing their studies, improving productivity, and reducing academic stress. The platform focuses on providing students with helpful tools and resources to support their academic journey.

Target Audience:

- University Students
- College Students
- First-Year Students
- Students needing academic support

---

## Website Goals

The main goals of the website include:

- Introduce UniBuddy to students
- Promote student productivity tools
- Encourage collaboration among students
- Provide academic support information
- Allow students to contact UniBuddy
