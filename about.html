About UniBuddy

UniBuddy is focused soley on college and university students and mainly first years to navigate their academics and mantain a good well-being during their first year.

Mission

UniBuddy's mission is to be helful in assisting students to excel academically through using creative tools and one of a kind features.

Vision

UniBuddy's vision is to become one of the best student productivity platforms and soon there after the leading student productivity platform for all college and university students and not just only first years.

Target Users

University and college students, first-years needing guidance, study groups and campus communities, and students wanting better organisation.
